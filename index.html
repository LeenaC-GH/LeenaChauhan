Astrology Is Cool

People all over the world have used astrology to guide their decisions for thousands of years. As the modern world becomes more tech-driven, interest in this time-tested subject has been growing. While technology can be used to serve humanity, there is also a growing awareness that not everything can be reduced to 1 and 0. Totally surrendering our brains to AI would be unwise. We are also creatures in need of connection to other sentient life, and to something beyond our own lives. Astrology and its ancient wisdom remind us to engage our curious minds, fertile imaginations and intuitive intelligence.

Aspects

Five key aspects describe the angular relationships between two or more planets used to interpret the meaning of planetary cycles.

  • Conjunction - 0 degrees
  • Sextile - 60 degrees
  • Square - 90 degrees
  • Trine - 120 degreees
  • Opposition - 180 degrees
